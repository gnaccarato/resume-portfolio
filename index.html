About Me

Getting started

I created my first blog website with Wordpress in 2017. That led to two ecommerce businesses with Amazon and Shopify. I gained experience with web design, learned SEO principles, utilized Google Adwords as well as Google Analytics and Facebook Pixel. I created email marketing campaigns and landing pages to build up a list of customers, and used Facebook and Instagram Ads for additional traffic. I really enjoyed creating my own projects so I decided to become a web developer and help create projects for other people.

Education

I have taken several online courses on web development but mostly learned from creating my own projects. While learning, I found as many online resources as I could. I have completed several web development courses from online resources including Codecademy, FreeCodeCamp, edX, Udemy, and more. I have also gone right to the source and read through the documentations for the programming languages and frameworks that I use. I am learning more every day.

Experience

I have experience creating web pages with HTML5 and styling them with CSS3. I know Javascript ES6 and I have also used React to create several web apps. I also know some back end development with Node and Express, MongoDB, and REST APIs. I use GIT and have experience with responsive design.

Other Skills

I am a fast learner and hard worker. My personality is easy going and I am a team player. I work well with others but I also work well on my own. I communicate effectively and have excellent reading and writing skills. I excel at producing solutions to solve problems and I always get the job done to meet deadlines.
